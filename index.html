Polio cases in Pakistan

A timeline

  • 2020: The year of Covid

    So far, the year has seen 64 cases of the wild polio virus in the country. Whereas, 52 cases of the vaccine-derived poliovirus type-2 (cVDPV2) have been confirmed this year.

  • 2019: When the cases began to multiply

    The year saw 147 cases of the wild polio virus and 22 cases of cVDPV2.

  • 2018: Cases on the rise

    2018 saw a total of 18 cases of the wild polio virus, whereas, there were no cases of vaccine derived polio.

  • 2017: Further drop in new cases

    A total of eight cases of the wild polio virus were reported this year and there were no cases of vaccine-derived polio.

  • 2016: Good tidings as significant drop witnessed in new cases

    This year saw 20 cases of the wild polio virus and 1 case of cVDPV2.

  • 2015: Pakistan has a challenge

    2015 saw 54 cases of the wild polio virus and a total of two cases of cVDPV2.
